About me

I'm Natália Mitsuko, a UX/UI Designer from Maceió, Brazil since 2021. My background in Psychology enhances my understanding of human behavior and digital interactions. With an MBA in UX Design and professional expertise since mid-2021, I specialize in user research, analysis, prototyping, and usability testing. My passion lies in creating accessible digital experiences that solve real user problems while driving business goals forward.
